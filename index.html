Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

✦
What is the dress code?+
We'd love for our guests to dress in formal or semi-formal attire — gowns, barong tagalog, or suits are perfect. Please avoid wearing white and ivory out of respect for the bride.
Can I bring my children?+
We adore your little ones! However, we have planned an intimate adults-only evening. We hope you understand — treat it as a wonderful night out for yourselves.
Is there parking available?+
Yes! Free parking is available on-site at Fernwood Gardens. For guests coming from farther away, a shuttle service from Seda Vertis North will run every 30 minutes starting at 2:30 PM.
What time should I arrive?+
Doors open at 3:00 PM and guests should be seated by 3:45 PM. The ceremony starts promptly at 4:00 PM — late entry will not be possible once it begins.
Are there accommodation options nearby?+
We recommend Seda Vertis North (~10 min drive) or The Bayleaf Intramuros (~25 min drive). Both are great for out-of-town guests. Book early as rooms fill up fast during the October long weekend.
Can I take photos during the ceremony?+
We're having an unplugged ceremony — no phones or cameras during the vows, please. We want everyone fully present in that moment. Our photographer will capture everything beautifully, and we'll share the full gallery after.
